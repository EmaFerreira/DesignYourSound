documentation

It is an experience in 4 stages:

  • 1st, you search for a sound that is part of the freesound API - we only provide you sounds with 0 Common Licenses, which means you can download and use them for free, without worrying about referencing their author.
  • 2nd, you explore and change the several sound properties by moving the sliders - you can read the properties' description by clicking on their label
  • 3rd, as we have your sound playing in a 4 beats loop, you can decide the tempo and volume as well as the beats on which the sound should play.
  • Finally, you can record your sound, changing it along the way - once you stop recording, your browser will automatically download it.

    DESIGN YOUR SOUND gives you a .webm file - you can convert it to .wav, .aiff or .mp3 format using online and free platforms such as Online Audio Converter.

  • YOU CAN RESET ALL THE PARAMETERS BY DOUBLECLICKING ON THE PAUSE BUTTON
