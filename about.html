about this project

DESIGN YOUR SOUND is a tool created with the aim to simplify the experience of sound design.

It is designed for those who want to find a sound file online and work it in a simple and quick way.

All the sounds provided by DESIGN YOUR SOUND are part of the Freesound API.

DESIGN YOUR SOUND only provides 0-Common Licenses sounds, which means all the soundfiles used inside this application are copyrights free.
